Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where programmers, designers, and other tech enthusiasts come together to collaborate on software projects within a limited time frame.

Who can participate?

Anyone with a passion for technology and innovation can participate, regardless of their skill level or background.

Do I need a team?

You can participate solo or form a team of up to 4 members. We'll also have team formation activities if you're looking to join a team.
